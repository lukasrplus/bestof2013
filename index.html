Experience 2013 with Lukas

  • Warhol/Basquiat Exhibition in Vienna

    Exhibition in Vienna that highlights the collaborative work of Andy Warhol and Jean-Michele Basquiat. It is very different to "classic" Warhol exhibitions because the focus is solely on paintings.

  • Attersee, Austria

    Definitely one of my favorite places in Austria. Especially in summer. Crystal clear water, a beuatiful scenery and winds that allow great sailing.

  • Titleist 712u

    The most beautiful golf club that was released in 2013. A classic shape combined with the playability that actually makes it fun to play.

  • Arzaga Golf Club, Lago di Garda, Italy

    Arzaga Golf Club in Italy is an amazing place and a really beautiful Golf course. I played there twice in 2013 and I am definitely coming back.

  • Gravity

    Great SIFI movie with only 1-2 actors. Very impressive in the cinema because of the great usage of space silence.

  • "Zum schwazren Kameel"

    "Zum Schwarzen Kameel" is a restaurant, bar and grocery store in Vienna. It was founded in 1613 and has a very decent atmosphere.

  • Summertime in Vienna

    Like every year the summer in Vienna was great. Warm evenings in parks with a bottle of wine and good friends are just unbeatable.

  • Kitsune Soleil 2

    My soundtrack for the summer. Kitsune is a French electronic music lable that I love for years. That mixtape is absolutely adorable.

  • Skiing World Championship 2013, schladming, Austria

    In February the skiing world championsships took place in Schladming, Austria. I went there for the men's downhill event and although there was no Austrian victory we had a great time and party there.
